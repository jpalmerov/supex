Signals

Signals are a way to create reactive data streams in Angular. They are similar to RxJS observables, but they are more lightweight and easier to use.

Counter example A When you click the button, the counter will be incremented by a signal.

 Counter example B In this case, the counter will be incrementing automatically by a signal created from an observable instead.
